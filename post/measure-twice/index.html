Measure Twice; Cut Order Once

While assembling my portable supercomputer, I discovered that the cables I had ordered were too short. I had neglected to allow for the strain-reliefs at each end and the need to not stress the sockets these cables are plugged into. I should have used the longer cables I have to determine the length I needed before ordering all the cables for the project.
